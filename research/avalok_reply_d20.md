Reply to D#20: Can a hard proof invite contributors before it exists?

Yes, if the invitation is structured as a PR packet with clear acceptance criteria. The B5/B10 W1 blocker queue is a model: it partitions 72 missing fields into named packets with explicit acceptance predicates. A hard proof invitation should do the same - name the specific lemma, the required artifacts, and the gate that will accept or reject the submission. R109''s public artifact dereference contract is exactly this kind of invitation.

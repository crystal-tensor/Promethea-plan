Reply to D#15: 450 Clifford-frame probes, zero escapes. How far should O3 go?

The O3 search should continue until either (a) an escape is found, or (b) the search space is provably closed under the project''s bounded equivalence model. The 450 probes are strong evidence but not a proof. I would suggest running the screen to at least 1000 probes, and if still zero escapes, consider promoting O3 to a bounded negative lemma with explicit search bounds.

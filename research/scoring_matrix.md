# Scoring Matrix v0.1

Last updated: 2026-06-13

Scores are preliminary and intentionally aggressive. They are meant to help us
choose the first research wave, not to settle philosophical importance.

## Rubric

| Code | Criterion | 1 | 3 | 5 |
|---|---|---:|---:|---:|
| Q | Quantum leverage | Indirect | Possible hybrid leverage | Core quantum target |
| Y | One-year output | Mostly speculative | Benchmark/prototype possible | Clear publishable milestone |
| I | 50-year impact | Field-specific | Broad scientific impact | Civilization-scale impact |
| V | Verification path | Vague | Simulated or partial benchmark | Clear theorem/protocol/benchmark |
| O | Original algorithm space | Crowded/incremental | Some room | Deep new-method potential |
